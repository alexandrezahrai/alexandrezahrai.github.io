Pig Game

Demo | Code

Who doesn't like having a little fun? The game of Pig is a very simple dice game in which two players battle to reach 100 points. Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player holds and scores the sum of the rolls. If a player holds, their turn total is added to their total score and it becomes the opponents turn.
